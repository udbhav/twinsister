Who are you guys?

Twin Sister is a band based in and around New York. Andrea Estella sings, Bryan Ujueta plays the drums, Eric Cardona plays guitar, Gabel D'Amico plays the bass, and Udbhav Gupta plays keyboard.

How do I download your songs?

Right click (Ctrl-Click for Macs) any song, and select "Save Link As" or the equivalent in your browser of choice.

I want to use your music in my own work.

All our music is licensed under Creative Commons, which means you should feel free to use anything here for non-commercial purposes. If you're interested in using our music for commercial purposes, please email us.

Who took that awesome photo on the top of your site?

Our friend, Lexis. He also goes by the name of The Popular Guru.

How can I get you guys to play a show?

Email us and ask us to play!

I work for a paper/magazine/website. I want to write something about you guys.

We love talking about ourselves. Email us.

I like your website, I want to make one like it for my band.

Thanks! Our website runs on Django, a web framework written in Python. Get the source code for the site on GitHub: http://github.com/udbhav/twinsister. If you have more questions, contact Udbhav via email: gupta.udbhav@gmail.com.
