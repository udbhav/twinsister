Who are you guys?

Twin Sister is a band based in and around New York. Andrea Estella sings, Bryan Ujueta plays the drums, Eric Cardona plays guitar, Gabel D'Amico plays the bass, and Udbhav Gupta plays keyboard.

How do I download your songs?

Right click (Ctrl-Click for Macs) any song, and select "Save Link As" or the equivalent in your browser of choice.

I want to use your music in my own work.

Please contact Domino Publishing through their website. In general, we allow free use of our work for non-commercial purposes, the kind folks at Domino Publishing would be glad to help answer any questions you have on the nature of the usage.

How can I get you guys to play a show?

For US booking contact Avery McTaggart at averymctaggart@theagencygroup.com. For everything else, contact us at band@twinsistermusic.com

I work for a paper/magazine/website. I want to write something about you guys.

Contact Hunter at Hunter@InfiniteBestRecordings.com. Could you do us a favor, and link to our website instead of myspace?

I like your website, I want to make one like it for my band.

Thanks! Our website runs on Django, a web framework written in Python. Get the source code for the site on GitHub: http://github.com/udbhav/twinsister. If you have more questions, contact Udbhav via email: udbhav@twinsistermusic.com.
